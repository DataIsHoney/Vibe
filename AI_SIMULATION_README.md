## ğŸ“ Pedagogical Approach

### Learning Through Storytelling
- Real business scenarios make concepts relatable
- Characters have authentic challenges and concerns
- Conversations build progressively from basic to advanced

### Guided Discovery
- Users learn by helping characters solve problems
- Concepts emerge naturally through dialogue

- Users make choices that drive conversations
- Immediate feedback through character responses
- Progress tracking creates sense of achievement

### Scaffolded Learning
- Simple explanations without jargon
- Business analogies instead of technical terms
- Concrete examples before abstract concepts
